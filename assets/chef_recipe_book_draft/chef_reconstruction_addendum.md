## Riz ma' Ful (رز مع فول)
[CHEF RECONSTRUCTION]
[CONFIDENCE: MEDIUM]

Rice cooked with green fava beans.

### Ingredients
- 2 cups short grain rice
- 1 cup fresh or frozen fava beans
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 tbsp butter or ghee
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1 tsp allspice
- salt

### Method
Sweat onion in butter. Add spices and rice.
Add fava beans and stock.
Cook covered until rice is tender.
Finish with browned butter and parsley.
