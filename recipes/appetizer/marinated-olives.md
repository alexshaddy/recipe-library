# Marinated Olives

> [!NOTE] Origin
> Clean handwritten olive marinade from notebook page 5.

## Ingredients

_Base servings: 1 batch_

- [ ] `2 cups` — olives
- [ ] `1/4 cup` — extra-virgin olive oil
- [ ] `2 tablespoons` — red wine vinegar
- [ ] `5` — bay leaves
- [ ] `1 large clove` — garlic, thinly sliced
- [ ] `1 tablespoon` — fresh thyme, chopped
- [ ] `1 tablespoon` — fresh rosemary, chopped
- [ ] `1 teaspoon` — fennel seed
- [ ] `1` — lemon zest
- [ ] `1/2 teaspoon` — ground clove

## Instructions

1. **Bloom the aromatics.** Heat the olive oil, vinegar, bay leaves, garlic, thyme, rosemary, fennel seed, lemon zest, and ground clove in a saucepan over low heat until the spices blossom and become fragrant, about 5 minutes.
2. **Combine with olives.** Pour the warm aromatic oil over the olives and toss to combine.
3. **Marinate.** Let the mixture sit for 48 hours, shaking occasionally so the flavors distribute.
4. **Serve.** Scoop the olives out with a slotted spoon and serve at room temperature.

## Notes & Variations

### Cook's Notes

- The ground clove is a warm accent, not a dominant flavor — use it sparingly.

### Make-Ahead / Storage

- Keep refrigerated and covered.

### Scaling

- The flavors get stronger as they sit, so a large batch should be tasted before service.
