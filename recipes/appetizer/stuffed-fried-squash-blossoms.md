# Stuffed Fried Squash Blossoms

> [!NOTE] Origin
> Notebook dinner note from page 22.

## Ingredients

_Base servings: 12 blossoms_

- [ ] `12` — squash blossoms
- [ ] `1 cup` — labneh (see [[labneh]] recipe)
- [ ] `1` — egg
- [ ] `1/2 cup` — flour
- [ ] `oil` — for frying
- [ ] `salt` — to taste

## Instructions

1. **Fill the blossoms.** Spoon a small amount of labneh into each blossom and twist closed.
2. **Batter and fry.** Dredge lightly, then fry until crisp and pale gold.
3. **Drain and serve.** Salt immediately and serve while the center is still creamy.

## Notes & Variations

### Cook's Notes

- The page reads like a fine-dining garnish note, so keep the blossoms small and delicate.

### Make-Ahead / Storage

- Fry right before serving.

### Scaling

- If the filling gets warm, chill it before stuffing.
