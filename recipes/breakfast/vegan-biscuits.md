# Vegan Biscuits

> [!NOTE] Origin
> Straight transcription from notebook page 1.

## Ingredients

_Base servings: 8 biscuits_

- [ ] `2 cups` — all-purpose flour
- [ ] `2 teaspoons` — baking powder
- [ ] `1 teaspoon` — salt
- [ ] `7 tablespoons` — non-dairy butter, cold
- [ ] `3/4 cup` — almond milk, cold
- [ ] `1 tablespoon` — lemon juice or cider vinegar


## Instructions

1. **Make the vegan buttermilk.** Stir the almond milk and lemon juice together and let it sit until slightly curdled.
2. **Combine the dry ingredients.** Whisk the flour, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
3. **Cut in the fat.** Work the cold non-dairy butter into the flour until the pieces are pea-sized.
4. **Form the dough.** Add the curdled milk and mix just until the dough comes together.
5. **Fold, cut, and bake.** Fold the dough a few times, cut straight down, and bake at 425 F until golden.

## Notes & Variations

### Cook's Notes

- The structure is the same as the dairy version; only the fat and liquid change.

### Make-Ahead / Storage

- Best served warm from the oven.

### Scaling

- Keep the ratio of flour to fat the same if you scale.
