## Ingredients

- [ ] `3 tablespoons` — salt
- [ ] `1 tablespoon` — paprika
- [ ] `2 tablespoons` — black pepper

---

## Instructions

1. **Load the blender.** Add everything except the chickpeas to a blender and process until smooth.
2. **Add the chickpeas.** Transfer the smooth base to a cambro with the chickpeas.
3. **Finish the texture.** Blend with a stick blender until the hummus is creamy and uniform.
4. **Adjust if needed.** Taste and loosen with a little water if the mixture is too thick.

---

## Notes & Variations

### Cook's Notes
- The scan reads like a professional kitchen formula, so the texture is the main thing to watch. Stop as soon as it turns smooth and silky.

### Variations
- **Roasted-garlic version:** The card already leans hard into roasted garlic; dial it back if you want a milder dip.

### Make-Ahead / Storage
- Refrigerate covered and use within about 4 days.
- Stir before serving if the oil separates slightly.

### Scaling
- This is a large-batch formula; if you scale it down, blend carefully so the motor still catches the chickpeas.
