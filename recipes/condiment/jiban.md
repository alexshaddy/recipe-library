# Jiban

> [!NOTE] Origin
> Pop-up prep notebook recipe from page 4.

## Ingredients

*Base servings: 1 batch*

- [ ] `3 gallons` — whole milk
- [ ] `1 1/2 cups` — whipping cream
- [ ] `1` — rennet tablet
- [ ] `as needed` — muslin or cheesecloth

## Instructions

1. **Warm the dairy.** Heat the milk and cream to 70 to 80 F without letting it boil.
2. **Add the rennet.** Dissolve the rennet tablet in cold water and stir it into the warm dairy.
3. **Set the curd.** Hold gently on low heat until the milk coagulates into a soft curd.
4. **Drain overnight.** Scoop the curds into cheesecloth, wrap into parcels, and let them drain in the refrigerator.

## Notes & Variations

### Cook's Notes
- The notebook suggests a richer cheese because cream is added to the milk base.

### Make-Ahead / Storage
- Chill overnight and salt once the cheese has drained.

### Scaling
- Keep the cream proportional if you reduce the batch.
