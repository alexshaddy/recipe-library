# Never-A-Lump Turkey Gravy

> [!NOTE] Origin
> Carnation recipe booklet page C-161.

---

## Ingredients

*Base servings: 8 servings [ESTIMATED]*

- [ ] `1/4 cup` — turkey drippings
- [ ] `1/4 cup` — seasoned flour
- [ ] `1 1/2 cups` — Carnation evaporated milk, undiluted
- [ ] `1 1/2 cups` — water

---

## Instructions

1. **Start the roux.** Blend the turkey drippings and seasoned flour in a heavy skillet over low heat until smooth.
2. **Add the liquid.** Gradually stir in the evaporated milk and water mixture.
3. **Cook until thick.** Stir constantly over low heat until the gravy is thickened and smooth, with no lumps.
4. **Serve.** Use immediately over turkey or other roasted poultry.

---

## Notes & Variations

### Cook's Notes
- The flour is already seasoned, so taste before adding extra salt.

### Variations
- **Chicken gravy:** Use chicken drippings in place of turkey drippings.

### Make-Ahead / Storage
- Gravy is best fresh, but can be held warm over very low heat with a little extra liquid stirred in as needed.

### Scaling
- Keep the drippings-to-flour ratio even so the roux stays smooth.
