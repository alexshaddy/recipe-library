# Shortrib Dry Rub

> [!NOTE] Origin
> Short dry-rub note from notebook page 14.

## Ingredients

*Base servings: 1 batch*

- [ ] `2 tablespoons` — brown sugar
- [ ] `1 tablespoon` — paprika
- [ ] `1 tablespoon` — salt
- [ ] `1 teaspoon` — black pepper
- [ ] `1 teaspoon` — garlic powder
- [ ] `1 teaspoon` — onion powder
- [ ] `1/2 teaspoon` — cayenne

## Instructions

1. **Mix the rub.** Stir everything together until the color is even.
2. **Apply.** Rub onto short ribs or other meat before roasting, grilling, or smoking.

## Notes & Variations

### Cook's Notes
- This is almost certainly a service-style shorthand, so the spice balance matters more than the exact grams.

### Make-Ahead / Storage
- Keep in a sealed jar away from heat and light.

### Scaling
- Brown sugar clumps if the room is humid, so break it up as needed.
