# Sweet Chili Sauce

> [!NOTE] Origin
> Best-effort transcription from notebook page 13.

## Ingredients

*Base servings: 1 batch*

- [ ] `1 cup` — sugar
- [ ] `1/2 cup` — vinegar
- [ ] `1/2 cup` — water
- [ ] `2 tablespoons` — chile flakes or chopped chiles [TO VERIFY]
- [ ] `2 cloves` — garlic, minced
- [ ] `1 teaspoon` — salt

## Instructions

1. **Simmer the base.** Combine everything in a saucepan and bring it to a gentle simmer.
2. **Reduce slightly.** Cook until the sauce looks glossy and lightly syrupy.
3. **Cool.** Let it cool before bottling or serving.

## Notes & Variations

### Cook's Notes

### Make-Ahead / Storage
