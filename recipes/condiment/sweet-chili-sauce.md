# Sweet Chili Sauce

> [!NOTE] Origin
> Notebook page 13 sauce note: ¼ cup rice vinegar, ¼ cup water, ¼ cup sugar, 1.5 tbsp sambal oelek, ½ tbsp cornstarch + 1 tbsp water slurry.

---

## Ingredients

*Base servings: 1 batch*

- [ ] `1/4 cup` — sugar
- [ ] `1/4 cup` — rice vinegar
- [ ] `1/4 cup` — water
- [ ] `1.5 tablespoons` — sambal oelek
- [ ] `1/2 tablespoon` — cornstarch
- [ ] `1 tablespoon` — water (for slurry)

---

## Instructions

1. **Combine Base Ingredients.** In a saucepan, whisk together sugar, rice vinegar, and water until the sugar dissolves. The mixture should look clear and thin.

2. **Add Sambal Oelek.** Whisk in the sambal oelek until fully incorporated. The sauce will turn a vibrant red color.

3. **Prepare Slurry.** In a small bowl, mix the cornstarch with 1 tablespoon of water until smooth — no lumps should remain.

4. **Simmer and Thicken.** Place the saucepan over medium heat. Bring to a gentle simmer, then slowly pour in the cornstarch slurry while whisking constantly. Continue to simmer, stirring frequently, until the sauce thickens and coats the back of a spoon (~8–10 min). It should be glossy and syrupy.

5. **Cool.** Remove from heat and let the sauce cool in the pan for 5 minutes, then transfer to a clean jar. It will thicken further as it cools.

6. **Store.** Seal and refrigerate. The sauce will keep for up to 2 weeks.

---

## Notes & Variations

### Cook's Notes
- Sambal oelek provides both heat and texture — adjust to taste if you prefer more or less spice.
- The cornstarch slurry gives the sauce its characteristic glossy, clingy texture without making it gloopy.

### Variations
- **Garlic-Chili Sweet Sauce:** Add 1 clove minced garlic with the sambal oelek in step 2 for a deeper aromatic profile.

### Make-Ahead
- Sweet chili sauce keeps refrigerated in an airtight container for up to 2 weeks.
- For longer storage, freeze in ice cube trays and transfer to a freezer bag for up to 3 months; thaw in refrigerator.

### Storage
- Store in a clean, sealed jar in the refrigerator for up to 2 weeks.
- Freeze for up to 3 months; thaw in refrigerator before use.

### Scaling
- The recipe scales linearly; maintain equal parts sugar, vinegar, and water (e.g., ½ cup each for double batch).
- Sambal oelek and cornstarch scale proportionally.
