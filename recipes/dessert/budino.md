# Budino

> [!NOTE] Origin
> Best-effort reconstruction from notebook page 15.

## Ingredients

*Base servings: 6 ramekins*

- [ ] `2 cups` — milk
- [ ] `1 cup` — cream
- [ ] `4` — egg yolks
- [ ] `1/2 cup` — sugar
- [ ] `2 tablespoons` — cornstarch
- [ ] `1 teaspoon` — vanilla
- [ ] `1 pinch` — salt

## Instructions

1. **Heat the dairy.** Warm the milk and cream until steaming.
2. **Thicken the custard.** Whisk the yolks, sugar, starch, and salt, then temper with the hot dairy and cook until thick.
3. **Chill.** Pour into ramekins and refrigerate until set.

## Notes & Variations

### Cook's Notes
- The notebook does not preserve a full formula, so the custard here is a conservative reconstruction.

### Make-Ahead / Storage
- Chill before serving and keep covered.

### Scaling
- Increase starch carefully if you want a firmer set.
