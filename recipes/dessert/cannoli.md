# Cannoli

> [!NOTE] Origin
> Best-effort transcription from notebook page 24.

## Ingredients

*Base servings: 12 cannoli*

- [ ] `12` — cannoli shells [ESTIMATED]
- [ ] `2 cups` — ricotta, drained
- [ ] `1/2 cup` — sugar
- [ ] `1 teaspoon` — vanilla
- [ ] `1/2 cup` — chocolate chips or candied fruit [ESTIMATED]
- [ ] `powdered sugar` — for finishing

## Instructions

1. **Make the filling.** Stir the ricotta, sugar, vanilla, and mix-ins together until smooth.
2. **Fill the shells.** Pipe the filling into the shells just before serving.
3. **Finish.** Dust with powdered sugar and plate immediately.

## Notes & Variations

### Cook's Notes
- The notebook places ricotta and cannoli together, which strongly suggests a home-style filling note.

### Make-Ahead / Storage
- Fill shells at the last minute so they stay crisp.

### Scaling
- The filling scales easily, but the shells do not like to sit once filled.
