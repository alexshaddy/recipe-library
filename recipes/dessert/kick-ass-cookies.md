# Kick-Ass Cookies

> [!NOTE] Origin
> Best-effort transcription from notebook page 15.

## Ingredients

*Base servings: 24 cookies*

- [ ] `2 cups` — flour
- [ ] `1 cup` — butter, softened
- [ ] `1 cup` — sugar
- [ ] `2` — eggs
- [ ] `1 teaspoon` — baking soda
- [ ] `1 teaspoon` — salt
- [ ] `1 teaspoon` — vanilla

## Instructions

1. **Cream the base.** Beat the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
2. **Build the dough.** Mix in the eggs and vanilla, then add the dry ingredients just until combined.
3. **Bake.** Drop the dough onto a sheet pan and bake until the edges are set and the centers are still soft.
