# Chimichurri Chicken Empanadas

> [!NOTE] Origin
> Best-effort transcription from notebook page 19.

## Ingredients

*Base servings: 12 empanadas*

- [ ] `2 cups` — cooked chicken, shredded
- [ ] `1/2 cup` — chimichurri
- [ ] `1` — onion, diced
- [ ] `12` — empanada discs or dough rounds
- [ ] `1` — egg, beaten, for sealing [ESTIMATED]

## Instructions

1. **Mix the filling.** Combine the chicken, chimichurri, and onion until well coated.
2. **Fill and seal.** Portion into dough rounds, fold, and crimp the edges tightly.
3. **Bake.** Cook until the pastry is browned and the filling is hot.

## Notes & Variations

### Cook's Notes
- Chimichurri tends to loosen fillings, so drain any excess liquid before assembling.

### Make-Ahead / Storage
- Assemble ahead and bake just before serving.

### Scaling
- Keep the filling dry enough to crimp cleanly.
