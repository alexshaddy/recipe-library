## Ingredients

*Base servings: 4 portions*

- [ ] `1 jar` — grape leaves, rinsed
- [ ] `1 cup` — rice
- [ ] `1/2 pound` — ground lamb or beef
- [ ] `1` — onion, minced
- [ ] `1 teaspoon` — allspice
- [ ] `1 teaspoon` — cinnamon
- [ ] `1 teaspoon` — salt
- [ ] `2 cups` — tomato broth [ESTIMATED]

## Instructions

1. **Mix the filling.** Combine the rice, meat, onion, and spices until the mixture is evenly seasoned.
2. **Roll the dolma.** Fill the leaves lightly and roll them into tight little parcels.
3. **Simmer gently.** Pack the rolls snugly in a pot, cover with broth, and cook until tender.
