# Empanada Dough / Biscuit Dough

> [!NOTE] Origin
> Shared dough note from notebook page 19.

## Ingredients

*Base servings: 1 batch*

- [ ] `3 cups` — flour
- [ ] `1 teaspoon` — salt
- [ ] `1 cup` — butter, chilled and cubed
- [ ] `1/2 to 3/4 cup` — cold water or milk [ESTIMATED]

## Instructions

1. **Cut in the fat.** Work the chilled butter into the flour and salt until the pieces are pea-sized.
2. **Form the dough.** Add cold liquid just until the dough comes together.
3. **Rest.** Chill before rolling so the dough relaxes and handles cleanly.

## Notes & Variations

### Cook's Notes
- The notebook seems to use one dough template for multiple uses, so this stays intentionally flexible.

### Make-Ahead / Storage
- Refrigerate wrapped or freeze portions for later.

### Scaling
- Add liquid slowly; the dough should not get wet.
