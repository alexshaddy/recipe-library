# Foie Gras

> [!NOTE] Origin
> Notebook dinner note from page 21.

## Ingredients

*Base servings: 2 portions*

- [ ] `1/2 pound` — foie gras
- [ ] `salt` — to taste
- [ ] `black pepper` — to taste
- [ ] `acid or fruit garnish` — as needed

## Instructions

1. **Prep the liver.** Trim and season the foie gras lightly.
2. **Sear.** Cook quickly in a hot pan until the exterior browns and the center stays lush.
3. **Serve immediately.** Plate with something acidic or sweet to balance the richness.

## Notes & Variations

### Scaling
