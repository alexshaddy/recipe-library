# Rooz M3 Ful / Ful

> [!NOTE] Origin
> Best-effort reconstruction from notebook page 9 and the addendum spelling notes.

## Ingredients

*Base servings: 4 portions*

- [ ] `2 cups` — short-grain rice
- [ ] `1 cup` — fava beans, fresh or frozen
- [ ] `1` — onion, diced
- [ ] `3 tablespoons` — butter or ghee
- [ ] `1 teaspoon` — cinnamon
- [ ] `1 teaspoon` — allspice
- [ ] `1 teaspoon` — salt

## Instructions

1. **Sweat the onion.** Cook the onion in butter until soft and fragrant.
2. **Build the rice.** Stir in the spices and rice so the grains are coated and toasted lightly.
3. **Finish with fava.** Add the fava beans and stock, then cook covered until the rice is tender and fluffy.

## Notes & Variations

### Cook's Notes
- The notebook title is the only solid clue, so the method follows a standard rice-and-fava family side dish.

### Make-Ahead / Storage
- Rewarm gently with a splash of water.

### Scaling
