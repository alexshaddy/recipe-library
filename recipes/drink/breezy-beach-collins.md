# Breezy Beach Collins

> [!NOTE] Origin
> Handwritten bar note from the inbox scan set. The title is reconstructed from the card’s description.

---

## Ingredients

*Base servings: 1 cocktail*

- [ ] `2 ounces` — white Martinique rhum, likely Clément white rhum
- [ ] `1 1/2 ounces` — ginger syrup
- [ ] `1/2 ounce` — lime juice
- [ ] `soda water` — to top
- [ ] `ice` — for serving
- [ ] `1` — lime wheel, for garnish

---

## Instructions

1. **Build the base.** Add the rhum, ginger syrup, and lime juice to a Collins glass over ice.
2. **Top with soda.** Add soda water to the top so the drink opens up and stays tall.
3. **Finish the Collins.** Stir gently and garnish with a lime wheel.

---

## Notes & Variations

### Cook's Notes
- The card describes this as a breezier, more beachy alternative to a hurricane, so keep it bright and refreshing.

### Variations
- **More tropical:** Add a small measure of pineapple juice if you want more of the hurricane impression.

### Make-Ahead / Storage
- Batch the rhum, ginger syrup, and lime juice in advance; add soda at service.

### Scaling
- The build scales well as long as the soda is held back until the glass is poured.
