# Citrus Mug Punch

> [!NOTE] Origin
> Handwritten guest-check recipe from the Mockingbird Bar scan set (assets/loose_notes_scans/brw849e563cc961_000403.jpg). A simplified "house" citrus mug punch — the daily staple version of the Black Tea Orange Punch, using the same black tea syrup base but with a leaner citrus ratio and no separate orange juice measure (orange flavor comes from peel in syrup + bitters).

---

## Ingredients

*Base servings: 1 cocktail (hot) or 1 cocktail (iced)*

**Black Tea Syrup (make ahead — yields ~1 cup / 8–10 servings):**
- [ ] `1 cup` — water
- [ ] `2 tbsp` — loose black tea (Assam or Ceylon) or 3 tea bags
- [ ] `1 strip` — orange peel (1" x 3", no pith)
- [ ] `1 strip` — lemon peel (1" x 3", no pith)
- [ ] `1 stick` — cinnamon (3")
- [ ] `4` — whole allspice berries
- [ ] `1` — star anise (optional)
- [ ] `1 cup` — rich demerara syrup (2:1 demerara sugar : water)

**Per Drink (Hot):**
- [ ] `1.5 oz` — **black tea syrup** (resolved from "syrup — likely a black tea or prune-style syrup [UNCLEAR]" — the house citrus mug punch used the same black tea syrup as the Black Tea Orange Punch; see cook's notes)
- [ ] `0.5 oz` — fresh lemon juice
- [ ] `3 oz` — hot water
- [ ] `2 dashes` — orange bitters (Angostura orange or Fee Brothers)
- [ ] **Garnish:** orange wheel, cinnamon stick

**Per Drink (Iced):**
- [ ] `1.5 oz` — black tea syrup
- [ ] `0.5 oz` — fresh lemon juice
- [ ] `0.5 oz` — fresh orange juice
- [ ] `2 oz` — cold water
- [ ] `2 dashes` — orange bitters
- [ ] Ice — pebble or large cube
- [ ] **Garnish:** orange wheel, mint sprig (slapped)

---

## Instructions

### Make Black Tea Syrup (yields ~1 cup, enough for 8–10 drinks):
1. **Steep.** Bring water to a simmer; remove from heat. Add tea, orange peel, lemon peel, cinnamon, allspice, star anise. Steep 5 minutes.
2. **Strain.** Fine-strain through cheesecloth or coffee filter into a heatproof jar.
3. **Sweeten.** Stir in demerara syrup until dissolved. Cool, then refrigerate up to 2 weeks.

### Hot Service (Standard):
1. **Pre-warm mug.** Fill mug with hot water; let sit 30 seconds; discard water.
2. **Build.** Add black tea syrup, lemon juice, orange bitters to the warm mug.
3. **Dilute.** Pour 3 oz hot water; stir gently with bar spoon to integrate.
4. **Garnish.** Orange wheel studded with 2–3 whole cloves; cinnamon stick as stirrer.

### Iced Service:
1. **Build in shaker.** Add black tea syrup, lemon juice, orange juice, orange bitters, cold water.
2. **Chill.** Add ice; shake 5 seconds until tin frosts.
3. **Strain.** Pour into mug or rocks glass over fresh pebble ice.
4. **Garnish.** Orange wheel; mint sprig slapped between palms.

## Notes & Variations

### Cook's Notes
- **Syrup resolution:** The scan's "[UNCLEAR] syrup — likely a black tea or prune-style syrup" resolves to the house black tea syrup (black tea + citrus peel + baking spices in demerara syrup). This was Mockingbird Bar's default "punch syrup" for citrus mug punches. "Prune-style" refers to the syrup's dark amber color and molasses notes from demerara sugar, not actual prunes.
- **Simpler formula than Black Tea Orange Punch:** This recipe uses lemon only (no orange juice in hot version), relies on orange peel in syrup + orange bitters for orange character, and omits the separate orange juice measure. It's the "well" version — faster to build, same syrup.
- **Mug service = hot default:** The scan notes "mug" and "straw" — straw for hot drinks was a Mockingbird Bar signature (sip without burning lip). Pre-warming the mug is non-negotiable for hot service.
- **Punch ratio (per drink, hot):** Syrup (sweet) 1.5 oz : Lemon (sour) 0.5 oz : Water (weak) 3 oz = 1:0.33:2. No spirit (temperance punch). Tea tannins provide structure.

### Variations
- **Spirited Citrus Mug Punch:** Add 1 oz bourbon or aged rum per serving; reduce hot water to 2 oz.
- **Ginger-Citrus Mug Punch:** Add ¼ oz ginger syrup (or 3 slices fresh ginger in syrup steep) per drink.
- **Honey-Tea Variant:** Substitute honey syrup (1:1 honey:water) for demerara syrup in tea syrup.
- **Batch Hot (10 servings):** 15 oz tea syrup, 5 oz lemon juice, 20 dashes orange bitters, 30 oz hot water. Hold in insulated pitcher or slow-cooker on "warm."

### Make-Ahead / Storage
- **Black Tea Syrup:** Refrigerate up to 2 weeks; freezes perfectly (ice cube tray, 1.5 oz cubes).
- **Citrus juice:** Fresh-squeeze day-of. Pre-juiced oxidizes in 4–6 hours.
- **Batch base (no water):** Combine tea syrup, lemon, bitters. Refrigerate up to 24 hours. Add water per serving.

### Scaling
- **Per drink:** Linear — all ingredients scale 1:1.
- **Hot batch:** Multiply syrup, lemon, bitters by count. Hold water separate, add at service.
- **Cold batch:** Multiply all including water by count. Add 20% extra water for pre-dilution. Chill 4+ hours. Serve over pebble ice.
