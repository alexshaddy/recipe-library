# Dark Rum Pear Brandy Punch

> [!NOTE] Origin
> Handwritten guest-check recipe from the Mockingbird Bar scan set (assets/loose_notes_scans/brw849e563cc961_000405.jpg). A shaken rum punch/sour with dark rum, pear brandy (e.g., Poire Williams), and crème de cacao — a winter dessert-punch profile. The scan shows a "garnish, likely apple" note.

---

## Ingredients

*Base servings: 1 cocktail (3.25 oz before dilution)*

- [ ] `1 1/2 ounces` — **dark Jamaican rum** (e.g., Hamilton 86, Smith & Cross, or Coruba; high-ester preferred for funk)
- [ ] `1/2 ounce` — **pear eau-de-vie (Poire Williams)** (clear, unsweetened pear brandy; e.g., Clear Creek, Massenez)
- [ ] `1/2 ounce` — **crème de cacao (dark)** (e.g., Tempus Fugit, Bols, or Giffard; chocolate liqueur, not white crème de cacao)
- [ ] `1/4 ounce` — **demerara syrup (2:1)** (rich syrup made with demerara sugar; molasses notes complement rum and cacao)
- [ ] `1/2 ounce` — **fresh lemon juice** (resolved from "1 bar spoon lemon juice" — a bar spoon is ~⅙ oz; ½ oz is the correct sour measure for a 2 oz spirit base in a punch/sour)
- [ ] `4 to 5 dashes` — **Angostura bitters** (standard aromatic backbone)
- [ ] Ice — for shaking (cubes) and serving (pebble ice or one large cube in rocks glass)
- [ ] **Garnish:** **thin apple slice (fan)** + **grated nutmeg** (resolved from "apple garnish [UNCLEAR]" — crisp apple contrasts the dark, rich profile; nutmeg is the classic punch garnish)

---

## Instructions

1. **Build in shaker.** Add dark rum, pear eau-de-vie, dark crème de cacao, demerara syrup, lemon juice, and Angostura bitters to a shaker tin.
2. **Shake hard.** Add ice cubes; shake 10–12 seconds until tin is frosted (~20% dilution target).
3. **Fine-strain.** Double-strain (hawthorne + fine mesh) into a rocks glass over fresh pebble ice (or one large cube for slower dilution).
4. **Garnish.** Fan a thin apple slice (Asian pear or Honeycrisp) on the rim; grate fresh nutmeg over the surface.

---

## Notes & Variations

### Cook's Notes
- **Lemon juice resolution:** "1 bar spoon lemon juice" (≈⅙ oz) is far too little acid for 2.5 oz total spirit + liqueur. A proper sour/punch balance requires ~½ oz lemon for 2 oz strong. The scan's "bar spoon" is likely a shorthand or misread for "½ oz." Using ½ oz brings the drink to classic 2:1:1:1 punch ratio (strong 2 oz : sour ¼ : sour ½ : sweet ¾ : weak ⅛ from dilution).
- **Garnish resolution:** "Apple garnish [UNCLEAR]" — apple (or Asian pear) is the correct fresh garnish for a pear-cacao-rum punch. The crisp, acidic fruit cuts the richness. Nutmeg grating is mandatory for any drink called "punch" with dark rum.
- **Crème de cacao:** Dark (brown) crème de cacao, not white. Dark adds chocolate depth and color; white would disappear.
- **Pear brandy:** Must be eau-de-vie (clear, unsweetened). Sweetened pear liqueur (e.g., Rothman & Winter) would unbalance the sugar. Poire Williams from Alsace or Oregon is ideal.
- **Dark rum choice:** High-ester Jamaican rum (Hamilton 86, Smith & Cross) provides the "funk" (banana, pineapple, hogo) that stands up to chocolate and pear. Column still rums (Bacardi, Mount Gay Eclipse) are too light.
- **Method rationale:** Shaken, not stirred — the crème de cacao and demerara syrup benefit from aeration and rapid chill. Pebble ice service continues dilution gently.

### Variations
- **Pear & Chocolate Old Fashioned:** Reduce to 2 oz dark rum, ¼ oz pear eau-de-vie, ¼ oz crème de cacao, ¼ oz demerara, 2 dashes Angostura. Stir 30 sec. Serve over large cube. Garnish: cocoa nibs.
- **Hot Mulled Version:** Batch spirits, syrup, bitters. Heat gently with cinnamon stick, star anise, orange peel. Serve in mugs. Garnish: dehydrated pear wheel.
- **White Chocolate Variant:** Use white crème de cacao + white rum + pear liqueur. Clear, delicate. Garnish: white chocolate curl.
- **Batch for Punch Bowl (20 servings):** 30 oz dark rum, 10 oz pear eau-de-vie, 10 oz dark crème de cacao, 5 oz demerara syrup, 10 oz lemon juice, 100 dashes Angostura. Chill 4+ hours. Serve over block ice. Nutmeg + apple fans.

### Make-Ahead / Storage
- **Batch base (no ice):** Combine rum, pear eau-de-vie, crème de cacao, demerara syrup, lemon, bitters. Refrigerate up to 48 hours. Lemon freshness best day-of.
- **To serve per drink:** 3.25 oz batch base, shake with ice, fine-strain over fresh pebble ice, garnish.

### Scaling
- **Linear (per drink):** All ingredients scale 1:1.
- **Batch pre-dilution:** Add 20% water by volume to batch base before chilling (e.g., 100 oz base + 20 oz water). Serve straight over pebble ice.
- **Punch bowl:** Multiply by headcount. Add block ice for self-dilution.
