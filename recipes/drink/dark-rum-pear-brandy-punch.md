## Ingredients

*Base servings: 1 cocktail*

- [ ] `1 1/2 ounces` — dark rum
- [ ] `1/2 ounce` — pear brandy
- [ ] `1/2 ounce` — crème de cacao
- [ ] `1/4 ounce` — demerara syrup
- [ ] `1 bar spoon` — lemon juice 
- [ ] `4 to 5 dashes` — Angostura bitters
- [ ] `ice` — for shaking and serving
- [ ] `1` — apple garnish, if using the original service style [UNCLEAR]

---

## Instructions

1. **Shake the punch.** Combine the rum, pear brandy, crème de cacao, demerara syrup, lemon juice, and bitters in a shaker with ice.
2. **Chill hard.** Shake until the drink is cold and the tin frosts.
3. **Serve on ice.** Strain into a rocks glass over fresh ice.
4. **Garnish.** Add the visible garnish once the original card is confirmed.
