# Life Preserver

> [!NOTE] Origin
> Transcribed from handwritten guest-check bar notes in the inbox scan set.

---

## Ingredients

*Base servings: 1 cocktail*

- [ ] `1 1/2 ounces` — vodka
- [ ] `1/2 ounce` — peach puree
- [ ] `1/2 ounce` — citrus juice, likely lemon or lime
- [ ] `1/4 ounce` — demerara syrup
- [ ] `1/4 ounce` — orange liqueur
- [ ] `5 dashes` — Angostura bitters
- [ ] `crushed ice` — for serving
- [ ] `1` — peach garnish, if desired

---

## Instructions

1. **Chill the shaker.** Add the vodka, peach puree, citrus juice, demerara syrup, orange liqueur, and bitters to a shaker with ice.
2. **Shake hard.** Shake until the outside of the tin frosts and the mixture is well chilled.
3. **Serve over crushed ice.** Fill a glass with crushed ice and strain the drink over it so it stays bright and slushy.
4. **Garnish.** Finish with a peach garnish if you have one.

---

## Notes & Variations

### Cook's Notes
- The note suggests a citrus-forward peach cocktail with a little bitters complexity; the exact citrus is unclear in the scan, so I left it generic.

### Variations
- **More tart version:** Use lime juice instead of lemon juice if you want a sharper finish.

### Make-Ahead / Storage
- Best made fresh; the crushed-ice texture fades quickly.

### Scaling
- Multiply the build directly for a batch, then shake individual servings to order.
