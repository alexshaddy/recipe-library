## Ingredients

*Base servings: 1 cocktail*

- [ ] `2 ounces` — rye or bourbon
- [ ] `1 ounce` — sweet vermouth
- [ ] `2 dashes` — bitters
- [ ] `1` — cherry, for garnish

## Instructions

1. **Stir.** Combine the whiskey, vermouth, and bitters with ice until well chilled.
2. **Strain.** Pour into a chilled glass and garnish with a cherry.
