# Old Fashioned

> [!NOTE] Origin
> Notebook cocktail note from page 18.

## Ingredients

*Base servings: 1 cocktail*

- [ ] `2 ounces` — whiskey
- [ ] `1 sugar cube` — or `1 teaspoon` simple syrup
- [ ] `2 to 3 dashes` — bitters
- [ ] `1 strip` — orange peel

## Instructions

1. **Build the drink.** Muddle the sugar with bitters and a splash of water if using a cube.
2. **Add the spirit.** Stir in the whiskey with ice until cold and slightly diluted.
3. **Garnish.** Express the orange peel over the drink and drop it in.
