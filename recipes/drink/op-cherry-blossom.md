# OP Cherry Blossom

> [!NOTE] Origin
> Page 12 cocktail note from the Mockingbird Bar spread.

## Ingredients

*Base servings: 1 cocktail*

- [ ] `2 ounces` — Old Grand-Dad bourbon
- [ ] `0.25 ounces` — sakura tea syrup
- [ ] `3 dashes` — Regans' orange bitters

## Instructions

1. **Stir with ice.** Stir the bourbon, syrup, and bitters until well chilled.
2. **Strain.** Pour into a coupe or rocks glass.

## Notes & Variations

### Cook's Notes
- The drink reads as a restrained cherry blossom riff, not a sweet cocktail.

### Make-Ahead / Storage
- Batch the base and add ice at service.

### Scaling
- Keep the syrup light so the bourbon stays front and center.
