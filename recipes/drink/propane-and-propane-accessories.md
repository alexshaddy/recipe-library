# Cucumber Watermelon Tequila Cocktail

> [!NOTE] Origin
> Working title for a handwritten bar note from the inbox scan set. The scan clearly shows a cucumber-infused tequila build with clarified watermelon and mint.

---

## Ingredients

*Base servings: 1 cocktail*

- [ ] `2 ounces` — cucumber-infused blanco tequila
- [ ] `1 ounce` — clarified acidified watermelon juice
- [ ] `1/2 ounce` — mint syrup
- [ ] `1/4 ounce` — cassis
- [ ] `1/4 ounce` — blanco mezcal
- [ ] `ice` — for shaking and serving

---

## Instructions

1. **Add the liquids.** Combine the tequila, clarified watermelon juice, mint syrup, cassis, and mezcal in a shaker with ice.
2. **Shake until cold.** Shake hard until the drink is bright, chilled, and lightly frothy.
3. **Serve.** Strain into a chilled glass or serve over fresh ice.
4. **Garnish lightly.** Keep the garnish minimal so the cucumber and watermelon notes stay crisp.

---

## Notes & Variations

### Cook's Notes
- The card reads like a very light, summery cocktail built around clarified watermelon and fresh mint.

### Variations
- **More vegetal:** Increase the cucumber element slightly.

### Make-Ahead / Storage
- The clarified watermelon component can be prepared ahead; shake the drink to order.

### Scaling
- This recipe scales well if the clarified juice is already prepped.
