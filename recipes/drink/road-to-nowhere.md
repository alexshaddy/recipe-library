# Road to Nowhere

> [!NOTE] Origin
> Transcribed from handwritten guest-check bar notes in the inbox scan set.

---

## Ingredients

*Base servings: 1 cocktail*

- [ ] `1 1/2 ounces` — rye whiskey
- [ ] `1/2 ounce` — cassis
- [ ] `1/2 ounce` — cinnamon syrup
- [ ] `1/4 ounce` — allspice liqueur
- [ ] `soda water` — to top
- [ ] `ice` — for serving

---

## Instructions

1. **Build the drink.** Add the rye, cassis, cinnamon syrup, and allspice liqueur to a glass over ice.
2. **Top with soda.** Add soda water until the drink is lively and lightly effervescent.
3. **Stir gently.** Give it one or two gentle turns so the spices stay bright and the drink stays tall.
4. **Serve cold.** Serve immediately as a spiced highball.

---

## Notes & Variations

### Cook's Notes
- The scan labels this as a spiced pear highball, but the written ingredient list is the rye-cassis-spice build shown here.

### Variations
- **Sweeter finish:** Add a small dash more cassis if you want more fruit.

### Make-Ahead / Storage
- Mix the non-carbonated portion in advance, but top with soda right before serving.

### Scaling
- The base build scales directly; keep the soda separate until service.
