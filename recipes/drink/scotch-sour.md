# Scotch Sour

> [!NOTE] Origin
> Notebook cocktail note from page 18.

## Ingredients

*Base servings: 1 cocktail*

- [ ] `2 ounces` — Scotch
- [ ] `3/4 ounce` — lemon juice
- [ ] `1/2 ounce` — simple syrup
- [ ] `1` — egg white [ESTIMATED]

## Instructions

1. **Shake the sour.** Shake all ingredients hard with ice until the tin feels frosty.
2. **Strain.** Pour into a chilled glass and serve immediately.

## Notes & Variations

### Cook's Notes
- The notebook name is enough to support a standard sour build.

### Make-Ahead / Storage
- Mix only when serving.

### Scaling
- The egg white is optional if you want a brighter, leaner drink.
