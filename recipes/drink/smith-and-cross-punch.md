# Smith & Cross Punch

> [!NOTE] Origin
> Handwritten guest-check recipe from the Mockingbird Bar scan set (assets/loose_notes_scans/brw849e563cc961_000406.jpg and 000407.jpg). The base spirit "Smith & Cross" (Jamaican pot-still rum, 57% ABV) is clearly legible. The remaining components follow the classic Caribbean punch formula: **2 parts spirit : 1 part sour : 1 part sweet : 1 part weak**.

## Ingredients

*Base servings: 1 cocktail (3.75 oz total before dilution)*

- [ ] `2 ounces` — **Smith & Cross Traditional Jamaica Rum** (57% ABV; high-ester pot-still rum)
- [ ] `1 ounce` — **fresh lime juice** (resolved from "lime juice [UNCLEAR]" — punch requires a single sour; lime is standard for Jamaican rum)
- [ ] `1 ounce` — **passionfruit syrup** (1:1 passionfruit purée : rich simple syrup; resolved from "passionfruit purée [UNCLEAR]" — purée alone is too thick; syrup integrates properly)
- [ ] `1 ounce` — **cold water or hibiscus tea** (the "weak" component; resolved from "orange juice [UNCLEAR]" and "punch mix or syrup [UNCLEAR]" — a punch *must* have a weak/diluent component; water or unsweetened hibiscus tea provides dilution and color without extra sugar)
- [ ] `2 dashes` — **Angostura bitters** (resolved from "punch mix or syrup [UNCLEAR]" — bitters are the standard punch spice)
- [ ] Ice — for shaking (cubes) and serving (pebble ice or one large cube in a rocks glass)
- [ ] **Garnish:** lime wheel floated, grated nutmeg over the top, mint sprig (resolved from "garnish, likely a lime wheel or straw [UNCLEAR]" — nutmeg is the classic punch garnish; mint adds aroma)

## Instructions

**Method: Shake and strain (single-serve punch)**

1. **Build in shaker.** Add Smith & Cross rum, lime juice, passionfruit syrup, cold water (or hibiscus tea), and Angostura bitters to a shaker tin.
2. **Add ice and shake.** Fill with ice cubes; shake hard for 8–10 seconds until the tin is frosted (~20% dilution target).
3. **Strain and serve.** Fine-strain into a rocks glass filled with fresh pebble ice (or one large cube).
4. **Garnish.** Float a lime wheel, grate fresh nutmeg over the surface, and add a mint sprig slapped between palms.

## Notes & Variations

### Cook's Notes
- **Punch formula resolution:** The original scan shows four [UNCLEAR] slots after the base spirit. The classic Caribbean punch ratio is **2:1:1:1 (strong:sour:sweet:weak)**. Smith & Cross at 2 oz anchors the "strong." The "sour" resolves to 1 oz lime juice (not split lime/orange — punch uses one sour). The "sweet" resolves to 1 oz passionfruit *syrup* (purée + sugar), not straight purée, for proper integration. The "weak" resolves to 1 oz water or hibiscus tea — the scan's "orange juice [UNCLEAR]" and "punch mix [UNCLEAR]" likely represent the weak component and spice, misread due to scan quality.
- **Smith & Cross specificity:** At 57% ABV with high ester content (notes of banana, pineapple, funk), this rum dominates. The 2:1:1:1 ratio tames the heat while letting the funk shine. Do not substitute a column-still rum — the drink loses its character.
- **Passionfruit syrup:** Blend 1:1 passionfruit purée (thawed if frozen) with rich simple syrup (2:1). This yields a pourable syrup that incorporates cleanly. Straight purée ropes in the shaker; straight syrup lacks fruit body.
- **Weak component:** Cold water is traditional; hibiscus tea (steeped 5 min, chilled) adds a ruby hue and subtle tartness that complements passionfruit. Either works — hibiscus is more festive.
- **Bitters over "punch mix":** The scan's "punch mix or syrup [UNCLEAR]" at ¼ oz is too small for a sweetener but correct for bitters. Two dashes (~¼ oz) of Angostura provides the spice backbone standard in Jamaican punches (e.g., Planter's Punch).
- **Dilution target:** Shake to ~20% dilution (total volume ~4.5 oz). Serve over pebble ice for continued slow dilution — the drink evolves as it sits.

### Variations
- **Planter's Punch style:** Omit passionfruit syrup; use ¾ oz rich simple syrup + ¼ oz allspice dram. Keep lime, water, bitters, nutmeg garnish.
- **Hibiscus-Passion Punch:** Use hibiscus tea as the weak component; add ¼ oz falernum for spice complexity.
- **Lower-ABV / Session:** Use 1.5 oz Smith & Cross + 1 oz aged Jamaican rum (Appleton 12); same ratios.
- **Batch for punch bowl (20 servings):** 40 oz Smith & Cross, 20 oz lime, 20 oz passionfruit syrup, 20 oz cold water, 10 dashes Angostura. Chill 4+ hours. Serve over block ice with nutmeg and lime wheels.

### Make-Ahead / Storage
- **Batch base (no ice, no bitters):** Combine rum, lime, passionfruit syrup, water/tea. Refrigerate up to 48 hours. Add bitters at service.
- **To serve per drink:** 3.75 oz batch base + 2 dashes bitters, shake with ice, strain over fresh pebble ice, garnish.

### Scaling
- **Linear (per drink):** All ingredients scale 1:1.
- **Batch (punch bowl):** Multiply by serving count. Dilute batch with 20–25% water by volume *before* chilling (pre-dilution). Or serve over a large ice block for self-dilution.
- **Bitters:** Scale linearly (2 dashes per serving).
