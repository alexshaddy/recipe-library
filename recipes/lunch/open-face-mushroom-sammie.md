# Open-Face Mushroom Sammie

> [!NOTE] Origin
> Best-effort transcription from notebook page 18.

## Ingredients

*Base servings: 2 sammies*

- [ ] `2 slices` — bread
- [ ] `2 cups` — mushrooms, sliced
- [ ] `1 tablespoon` — butter or oil
- [ ] `1 clove` — garlic, minced
- [ ] `1 tablespoon` — herbs [TO VERIFY]
- [ ] `salt` — to taste

## Instructions

1. **Toast the bread.** Crisp the bread so it can support the topping.
2. **Cook the mushrooms.** Sauté the mushrooms with butter, garlic, and herbs until browned and fragrant.
3. **Assemble.** Pile the mushrooms onto the toast and serve immediately.

## Notes & Variations

### Cook's Notes
- The notebook title suggests a simple hot sandwich, so this stays intentionally spare.

### Make-Ahead / Storage
- Best assembled at the last minute.

### Scaling
- Make the mushroom topping in small batches so it browns instead of steaming.
