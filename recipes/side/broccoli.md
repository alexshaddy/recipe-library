# Broccoli

> [!NOTE] Origin
> Best-effort reconstruction from notebook pages 21 and 22.

## Ingredients

*Base servings: 4 portions*

- [ ] `2 heads` — broccoli, cut into florets
- [ ] `2 tablespoons` — olive oil
- [ ] `2 cloves` — garlic, sliced
- [ ] `1 tablespoon` — butter [ESTIMATED]
- [ ] `salt` — to taste
- [ ] `lemon juice` — to finish [ESTIMATED]

## Instructions

1. **Brown the aromatics.** Heat the oil and garlic until fragrant.
2. **Cook the broccoli.** Add the broccoli and toss until the florets turn bright green and lightly charred in spots.
3. **Finish.** Add butter, salt, and lemon just before serving.

## Notes & Variations

### Cook's Notes
- The notebook page reads like a plating note, so this is intentionally simple.

### Make-Ahead / Storage
- Best eaten right away.

### Scaling
- Avoid overcrowding or the broccoli will steam instead of browning.
