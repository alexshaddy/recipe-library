# Curry Lentil Soup

## Ingredients

*Base servings: 4 bowls*

- [ ] `1 cup` — lentils
- [ ] `1` — onion, diced
- [ ] `2 cloves` — garlic
- [ ] `1 tablespoon` — curry powder
- [ ] `4 cups` — stock or water
- [ ] `1 tablespoon` — oil
- [ ] `1 teaspoon` — salt

## Instructions

1. **Build the base.** Sweat the onion and garlic in oil until fragrant.
2. **Simmer the soup.** Add the lentils, curry powder, stock, and salt, then cook until the lentils are tender.
3. **Finish.** Blend if desired and adjust the seasoning before serving.
