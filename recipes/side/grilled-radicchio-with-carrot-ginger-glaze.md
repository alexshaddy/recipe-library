# Grilled Radicchio with Carrot-Ginger Glaze

### Carrot-Ginger Glaze

- [ ] `1` — carrot

### Garnish

- [ ] `nori` — as needed

## Instructions

1. **Make the glaze.** Blend or process the carrot, ginger, sugar, vinegars, and sesame oil until smooth, then reduce to a glaze-like consistency if needed.
2. **Grill the radicchio.** Brush the halves with oil and grill cut-side down until the edges char and the centers soften slightly.
3. **Plate and garnish.** Spoon on the glaze and finish with the nori, sesame, and mushroom powder notes.
