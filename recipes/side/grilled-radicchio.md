# Grilled Radicchio

> [!NOTE] Origin
> Best-effort transcription from notebook page 24.

## Ingredients

*Base servings: 4 portions*

- [ ] `2 heads` — radicchio, halved
- [ ] `2 tablespoons` — olive oil
- [ ] `1 teaspoon` — salt
- [ ] `1 tablespoon` — balsamic or lemon [ESTIMATED]

## Instructions

1. **Oil and season.** Coat the radicchio lightly with oil and salt.
2. **Grill.** Cook over high heat until the leaves char and the cut edges caramelize.
3. **Finish.** Add acid right before serving.

## Notes & Variations

### Cook's Notes
- Keep the core intact so the wedges hold together on the grill.

### Make-Ahead / Storage
- Best served warm.

### Scaling
- Grill in a single layer.
