# Hind-Seh Mi'lia

> [!NOTE] Origin
> Reconstruction from the notebook title and addendum spelling notes.

## Ingredients

*Base servings: 4 portions*

- [ ] `1 pound` — dandelion greens
- [ ] `2` — onions, sliced
- [ ] `3 tablespoons` — olive oil
- [ ] `1/4 cup` — lemon juice
- [ ] `1/4 cup` — pine nuts
- [ ] `1 teaspoon` — salt

## Instructions

1. **Brown the onions.** Cook the onions slowly until soft, sweet, and deeply golden.
2. **Wilt the greens.** Add the dandelion greens and cook just until they collapse and turn glossy.
3. **Finish.** Season with lemon and salt, then top with pine nuts before serving.

## Notes & Variations

### Cook's Notes
- This is the kind of dish that benefits from a little bitterness left intact.

### Make-Ahead / Storage
- Serve warm or room temperature.

### Scaling
- The greens shrink a lot, so start with more than you think you need.
