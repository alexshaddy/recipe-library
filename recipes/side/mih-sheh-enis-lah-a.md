# Mih-Sheh / Enis & Lah'a

> [!NOTE] Origin
> Title-only notebook page, reconstructed as a stuffed-vegetable dish.

## Ingredients

*Base servings: 4 portions*

- [ ] `4` — zucchini or small squash
- [ ] `1 cup` — rice
- [ ] `1/2 pound` — ground lamb
- [ ] `1` — onion, minced
- [ ] `1 teaspoon` — cinnamon
- [ ] `1 teaspoon` — allspice
- [ ] `1 teaspoon` — salt
- [ ] `2 cups` — tomato broth [ESTIMATED]

## Instructions

1. **Prep the vegetables.** Hollow the squash and set the shells aside.
2. **Mix the filling.** Combine the rice, lamb, onion, and spices until evenly distributed.
3. **Stuff and simmer.** Fill the vegetables and cook them gently in tomato broth until tender.
