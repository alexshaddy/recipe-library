# Tabbouleh

> [!NOTE] Origin
> Best-effort reconstruction from notebook page 8.

## Ingredients

*Base servings: 1 batch*

- [ ] `4 cups` — parsley, finely chopped
- [ ] `1 cup` — tomatoes, diced
- [ ] `1/4 cup` — fine bulgur
- [ ] `1/4 cup` — scallions, minced
- [ ] `1/4 cup` — lemon juice
- [ ] `1/4 cup` — extra-virgin olive oil
- [ ] `1 teaspoon` — salt

## Instructions

1. **Soften the bulgur.** Soak the bulgur briefly if it needs to hydrate, then squeeze off any excess water.
2. **Toss the salad.** Combine the parsley, tomatoes, scallions, bulgur, lemon juice, oil, and salt.
3. **Adjust and rest.** Taste for brightness and salt, then let the salad sit so the flavors marry.

## Notes & Variations

### Cook's Notes
- The reconstruction keeps parsley as the lead ingredient, which is the usual family-style direction.

### Make-Ahead / Storage
- Best the day it is made; the herbs soften as it sits.

### Scaling
- Add bulgur conservatively when scaling up.
