# Yufka

> [!NOTE] Origin
> Best-effort reconstruction from notebook page 7 and the addendum.

## Ingredients

*Base servings: 8 pieces*

- [ ] `500 grams` — flour
- [ ] `325 grams` — water
- [ ] `10 grams` — salt

## Instructions

1. **Mix the dough.** Combine the flour, water, and salt into a smooth dough.
2. **Rest and roll.** Let the dough relax, then roll it extremely thin.
3. **Cook quickly.** Cook on a hot skillet or griddle until the bread sets and spots lightly.
